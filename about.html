About Me

I got into music production because I loved shaping sound since I was a kid. I'm a bedroom producer who enjoys dub and atmospheric techno, Detroit techno, and all kinds of inspiring electronic music. With a passion for sound design and production, I started this site to share simple tools and practical knowledge for beginners starting their journey. One of those tools is the delay and reverb calculator—just a small way to make learning and creating music a little easier.
