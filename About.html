About Me

I got into music production because I loved shaping sound since I was a kid. I'm a bedroom producer who’s passionate about dub techno, Detroit-inspired grooves, ambient textures and sound design.

I'm not a professional, just someone who enjoys making music and learning along the way. That’s why I built this site — to help newcomers understand timing, reverb, and delays more intuitively with useful tools like this calculator.

If you're starting out and find this helpful, then we’re already on the same wavelength.
